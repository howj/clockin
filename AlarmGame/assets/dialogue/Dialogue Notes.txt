Do that conversation and you get a super powerup.
Reach 0 and you get something really bad.

+ or a -, a letter indicating which character,
 and the number of points that they add or subtract.
These should be paired with some kinda artistic indication (visual and/or sound) that indicates
that the person either did or did not like what you said.

A small menu that shows bars of how much a character likes you will be accessible via a button
during free walk around time.

player gets for the next puzzle are marked with a #
